Give

St. Matthew Missionary Baptist Church appreciates your generosity and support. Your contributions help us continue our mission and serve our community. You can give for Tithes/Offerings, Annual Days, or the Mission Fund.

To give, you can use one of the following methods:

  1. In-Person: You can give during our Sunday service's offering time.
  2. Mail: Send your check or money order to:
    St. Matthew Missionary Baptist Church
    1826 Conti St.
    New Orleans, LA 70112
  3. Zelle: You can send your donation via Zelle to our church's email: st.matthew1826@yahoo.com.
  4. Cash App: You can also use Cash App to send your donation to $stmatthewmbc1826.
